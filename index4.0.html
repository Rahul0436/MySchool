Johny Johny, Yes Papa

Johny Johny, yes Papa,

Eating sugar? No, Papa.

Telling lies? No, Papa.

Open your mouth! Ha, ha, ha!

Johny Johny, up you go,

Climbing shelves? Oh no, no, no!

Are you jumping? Not at all!

Then what’s that sound outside the hall?

Johny Johny, what’s that there?

Chocolate smudges everywhere!

Did you sneak it? Who, me? No!

Time to clean, my little pro!
